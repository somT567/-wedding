How Mercy Found Us

Chisom:
I wasn’t looking for love. Not even close.

It started like nothing… just a moment, a conversation I didn’t think would matter.
And then life happened—I was disappointed, a bit guarded… just trying to move on quietly.

But somehow, you stayed.


Emeka:
I didn’t plan it either.

But there was something about you I couldn’t ignore.
Even when it would have been easier to walk away, I knew I didn’t want to.

So I stayed… not loudly, not perfectly… just intentionally.


Chisom:
You showed up in a way I didn’t expect.
Not just with words—but with presence.

That night… when you came all the way just to make me smile…
it felt simple, but it meant everything.

A movie. Quiet laughter. Peace.

And somewhere in that moment… I felt safe again.


Emeka:
I knew before I said it.

But when I finally did—“I love you”—
it didn’t feel sudden.
It felt like something that had always been waiting for you.


Chisom:
And somehow… I already knew too.


Together:
Six months later,
what started as something unexpected
became something undeniable.

This isn’t just our story.

It’s a reminder that love finds you…
even when you’re not looking.

And for us,
it came wrapped in mercy.
